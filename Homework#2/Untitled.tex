\subsubsection{SUMMARY}\label{summary}

In this experiment, we will try to prove the fairness of a die that was
heated in an over for 20 minutes. We want to claim that this die is not
fair. • We first have null hypothesis to make claim that our dice is
normal. And we try to prove this claim wrong. o Null Hypothesis: The die
is fair. The probability of getting 6 in one roll is 1/6 (P(getting 6) =
1/6) • We then have alternative hypothesis which if proven right will
contradict our null hypothesis. This will disprove that the dice is
normal. o Alternative Hypothesis: P(getting 6) != 1/6 • We have random
variable for the experiment. o Random variable: The number of times we
get 6. • Here we know that probability of getting 6 if the dice is fair.
It is 1/6. We also want to see true or false for getting an outcome.
